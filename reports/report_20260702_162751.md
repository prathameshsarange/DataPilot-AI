*   **Poor Code Explanation:** Being unable to clearly explain your code or project architecture.
*   **Not Researching the Company:** Showing up without knowing what the company does or what its values are.
*   **Over-promising:** Claiming to know technologies you've only briefly touched upon. Be honest about your skill level, but emphasize your eagerness to learn.

### Interview Tips

*   **Research the Company:** Understand their products, mission, and recent news. Tailor your answers to align with their goals.
*   **Behavioral Question Framework:** Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your answers for behavioral questions.
